Easy Baked Chicken Thighs

These easy baked chicken thighs are juicy, flavorful, and require minimum prep! I'm a college student and love this simple recipe because it's so versatile—season them any way you like for a satisfying meal every time.

Need a quick and easy dinner? Try this quick and easy baked chicken thighs recipe! It is simple to make with just a few ingredients and versatile enough for any night of the week — just season and bake.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 4 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 teaspoons dried minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken thighs in a baking dish; season both sides with garlic powder, dried minced onion, and salt.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink at the bone and juices run clear, about 45 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into thickest part of thigh, near the bone, should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
